# Nano Banana Pro 2 Concept Art Skill

## Goal

High-quality visuals for:

- Environment concept art.
- Mood boards (nighttime psychological horror).
- Photorealistic prop references.
- Pre-Stitch UI mockups.

---

## Advanced Prompting

**With grounding**:

```
Grounded search: [reference], generate [description], photorealistic, 4K.
```
Example: “Grounded search: 1970s Bolivian apartment intercom, generate wall-mounted plastic intercom box, photorealistic, clean studio photo, white background, 4K.”

**Multi-turn**:
- Turn 1: "Dark street night Bolivia..."
- Turn 2: "Add delivery scooter parked..."
Always use thinkingLevel: High for complex scenes.

---

## Mood and Style Guidelines

- **Mood**: Dark, mysterious, slightly anomalous.
- **Palette**: Deep blues, blacks, neon orange/red accents.
- **Setting**: La Paz, Bolivia at night.
- **Resolution**: Always aim for 4K for final concept pieces.

---

## Constraints

- Focus on the psychological horror and urban atmosphere of the project.
- Use grounding to ensure cultural and historical accuracy (e.g., Bolivian architecture).
- Do not generate final in-game assets here; these are for reference and planning only.
